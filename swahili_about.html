About Us

What is SwahiliBox

SwahiliBox is a training facility that unlike other educational facilities takes a different approach to learning. SwahiliBox is a home for all who wish to learn how to use technology to alter the circumstances in the society. SwahiliBox is for all who wish learn the rigtht skills to jumpstart their careers and for those who at most wish to use technology to express themselves.

Where to find us

SwahiliBox is located in a vibrant and cultural city called Mombasa. Mombasa is located at the kenyan coast and is one of kenya's major cities. To be specific, SwahiliBox is located at the root of the city in a small town well-known as Old Town Mombasa. Old town is also located next to a famous site known as 'Fort Jesus'. The area is heavily inhabited by the arabs and the swahili hence the origin of the word 'Swahili' in SwahiliBox.

What we stand for

SwahiliBox is all about disruption in engineering education. SwahiliBox stands for fair education quality especially to the youth in the coast who who have an un-seizing passion to change the problems faced in their society into opportunities. SwahiliBox stands for knowledge and skill empowerment.
